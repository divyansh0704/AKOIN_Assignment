About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to building the next generation of talent in digital, cyber, and technology risk management. We bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application through strategic partnerships with universities and direct involvement in live projects.

Our mission is to create a pipeline of job-ready professionals equipped to handle the complexities of the modern risk landscape, from AI governance to critical infrastructure protection.

Our Service Pillars

Academy
